Viktor Leis

Last Updated: Mär 28, 2021

Prof. Dr.

Bio

I am a full professor leading the databases and information systems group at Friedrich Schiller University Jena. My research revolves around designing efficient database systems and includes core database topics such as query processing, query optimization, transaction processing, index structures, and storage.
